Instructions

1. Find the contract address for the token you wish to share between multiple wallets.

2. Copy the contract address you are willing to share.

3. Click the approve button and fire the transaction and wait for it to confirm.

4. Use a new line for each wallet address and its respective amount of WQOM.

5. Input the total number of tokens that you are sharing as a collective total in the "Tokens To share" field.

6. The qomTRIBUTE dapp will calculate each wallets share of tokens according to the formula: QOM From Wallet/Total QOM Sum.

7. Click the SHARE button to start the transaction.

8. All tokens should be distributed accordingly once the transaction confirms.

9. Check on qomscan to confirm the distribution was successful.
